Our Story

Founded in 1995 by Chef Giovanni Rossi, Bella Tavola brings the authentic taste of Italy to your table. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, using only the freshest ingredients imported directly from Italy.

Every dish is prepared with love and passion, just like nonna used to make. From our wood-fired pizzas to homemade pastas, we invite you to experience true Italian hospitality.
